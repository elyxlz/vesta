## Amphora Wines (Ancient Winemaking Techniques)

### Italian Producers Using Clay Amphorae/Terra-cotta:

1. **Gravner (Friuli-Venezia Giulia)**
   - Pioneer of amphora winemaking revival in Italy
   - Ribolla Gialla white wines fermented/aged in clay amphorae
   - Some buried underground, minimal intervention
   - Look for UK imports/specialist retailers

2. **COS (Sicily - Cerasuolo di Vittoria)**
   - Uses terra-cotta amphorae for fermentation/aging
   - Abandoned oak/steel for pure terroir expression
   - Southern Italy amphora wines

3. **Tenuta Casadei - Le Anfore (Tuscany)**
   - Elena Casadei uses ancient amphorae (imported from Georgia)
   - Sangiovese, Syrah, Cannonau in amphorae
   - Modern winemaking with ancient vessels

### Shopping Notes:
- Look for labels: "in amphora", "terra-cotta amphorae", "anfora", "aged/fermented in clay"
- Check natural wine/low-intervention specialists
- Ask for heritage/traditional winemaking importers
- Expect premium pricing and unique styles (orange wines, textured reds)
