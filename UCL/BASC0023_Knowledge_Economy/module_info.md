## Project Overview
Consultancy project with Aspex Portsmouth (charity cultivating art and creativity, rooted in community) focusing on **enhancing the organisation's capacity for fundraising**.

**See aspex_project_brief.md for complete official project brief**

### Project Purpose
Support activities undertaken by CEO, board and freelance business development consultants. Student recommendations will be used for organization's fundraising action planning.

### Core Tasks
- Analyzing existing funder data (no primary research required)
- Preparing summaries of funder requirements
- Making recommendations for more effective, creative and ethical fundraising approaches
- Secondary research on capital funding for environmental improvements in Grade II listed buildings

### Current Fundraising Priorities (choose one or more)
1. **Core funding for staff**
2. **Capital project for environmental sustainability** in Grade II listed building
3. **Funding to change diesel mobile art studio** to electric alternative
4. **Community Engagement Programmes**
5. **Artistic Commissions**
6. **Early-stage art and energy project**

### Expected Deliverable
**Strategy document for fundraising** with:
- Range of funding sources with clear justification
- Focus on multi-year grants exceeding £10,000/year
- May include draft proposals or key messaging
- Mix of 'quick-wins' and substantial proposals

- **Past Success**: Successful crowdfunding for Generate programme, longlisted for Museum of the Year Award
