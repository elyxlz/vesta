# Funder Research: Similar Organizations Analysis

*Research conducted: October 22, 2025*
*Following Leo Burtin's advice: "spying on other similar organisations - whose logos are they showing on their websites / marketing materials?"*

## Research Methodology

Investigated contemporary art galleries and organizations in the UK with similar missions to ASPEX Portsmouth, focusing on:
- Community engagement programs
- Contemporary art programming
- Social justice/refugee work
- Independent charity status

## Key Funding Bodies Identified

### Primary Funders (appearing across multiple organizations)

**1. Arts Council England** 🎯
- **Ubiquitous presence** across all contemporary galleries
- **Grant Programs:**
  - National Lottery Project Grants (up to £100,000)
  - Developing Your Creative Practice (DYCP) fund
  - Museum Renewal Fund (£20 million total)
  - Creative People and Places program
- **Multi-year potential:** National Portfolio Organisation status
- **Perfect alignment:** Community engagement, diversity, accessibility

**2. Local Authority Support** 🎯
- **Bristol City Council** → Arnolfini model
- **Pattern:** Ongoing support relationships
- **For ASPEX:** Portsmouth City Council, Hampshire County Council

**3. University Partnerships** 🎯
- **UWE & Arnolfini** partnership model
- **Opportunities for ASPEX:**
  - University of Portsmouth
  - Solent University
- **Benefits:** Funding + programming + student engagement

### Specialized Funders

**4. Henry Moore Foundation**
**Website:** https://www.henry-moore.org
- **Focus:** Sculpture and contemporary art
- **Programs:**
  - Grants and fellowships
  - Young artist support
  - Exhibition support
- **Relevance:** Contemporary art with community focus ⭐⭐⭐⭐

**5. Ashley Clinton Barker-Mills Trust**
- **Currently supports:** Arnolfini Bristol
- **Focus:** Contemporary arts with community engagement
- **Target for ASPEX:** Direct approach ⭐⭐⭐⭐

## Strategic Insights

### Long-term Funding Patterns (5-10 years)

1. **Arts Council England NPO status** = Multi-year core funding
2. **Local authority partnerships** = Ongoing annual support
3. **University collaborations** = Long-term institutional relationships
4. **Major foundation relationships** = Multi-year project cycles

### Refugee/Community Work Funding

**Research shows organizations working with refugees use:**
- Corporate partnerships
- Community foundation grants
- Trust and foundation funding
- Individual donor programs
- Legacy giving
