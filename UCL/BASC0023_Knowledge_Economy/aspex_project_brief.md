# UCL Arts and Sciences (BASc) - BASc The Knowledge Economy: Student Consultancy Project

## i. Company Name and Description

**Aspex** is a charity cultivating art and creativity, rooted in community. We believe that in bringing joy, art broadens minds, facilitates learning, connects and nurtures our communities and stimulates radical imagining.

### Core Values
- **Communal**
- **Open-hearted**
- **Radical**

### Mission & Objectives
We strive to:
- **Explore urgent themes and issues** that are relevant to our communities through creative programming and commissioning, platforming artists who are underrepresented (in the arts) and marginalised
- **Connect and empower** artists, audiences and participants through creativity and collaboration
- **Be more inclusive, diverse and socially relevant**, actively living our values
- **Ensure environmental responsibility, financial sustainability** and be an open-hearted organisation

### Organizational Pride
Threaded through all strands of our work and organisation is a deep sense of pride. We take pride in Portsmouth, a resilient working-class city and a space of Sanctuary for those in need and we are proud of our diverse communities and the way we embed community engagement in all that we do.

## ii. Purpose of the Student Consultancy Project

**Objective:** To enhance the organisation's capacity for fundraising.

The students' work will be supporting the activities undertaken by the CEO, the board and freelance business development consultants on behalf of the charity. Students' recommendations will be utilised as part of the organisation's action planning pertaining to fundraising.

### Indicative Tasks
- **Analysing existing funder data** (no primary research required)
- **Preparing summaries of funder requirements**
- **Making recommendations** of how Aspex can approach fundraising more effectively, creatively and ethically
- **Undertaking secondary research** on securing capital funding to support making environmental improvements in Historical Buildings (Listed Buildings), taking into account the legislative requirements of such projects

*These will be discussed in more detail at the start of the project.*

## iii. Scope and Parameters of the Project

### Current Funding Structure
The organisation's core costs are covered by:
- **Arts Council England** via the National Portfolio programme
- **Portsmouth City Council**

Additional fundraising from:
- Range of private trusts and foundations
- Individual donors including regular patrons
- Bespoke public funds (such as National Heritage Lottery Funding, ArtFund etc.)

### Current Fundraising Priorities
1. **Core funding for staff**
2. **Capital project to improve environmental sustainability** in our Grade II listed building (historical building), which could therefore include research on how historical buildings can be heated in an environmentally friendly manner
3. **Funding for the change of Diesel mobile art studio** to an electric alternative
4. **Community Engagement Programmes**
5. **Artistic Commissions**
6. **An early-stage art and energy project**

*The student group may select from one or more of these priorities to develop their strategy and recommendations*

## iv. Expected Deliverables / Success Criteria

### Primary Deliverable
**A strategy document for fundraising for the organisation**, identifying the range of sources the organisation could apply for, with clear justification of why the selected funders / fundraising activities are:

a) **Appropriate for Aspex**
b) **Aligned with funder priorities**
c) **Realistically achievable** within the Aspex mission / business plan

### Additional Components
- The document may also include **draft proposals or key messaging** for specific funders
- The strategy would outline a range of funding opportunities suitable for the charity including **'quick-wins' and more substantial proposals**
- **Focus:** Trusts and foundations willing to offer **multi-year grants in excess of £10,000 per year**

## v. Technical Requirements

**Available Resources:**
- Aspex has a **database of funders** we have previously applied to, with notes on amounts, success rates and feedback that can be analysed
