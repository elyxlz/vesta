Dear new and returning BAScers,



Now that the 2025/26 academic year has formally begun, please find attached a copy of this year’s BASc student handbook. You can also view this on the DT Moodle page at https://moodle.ucl.ac.uk/course/view.php?id=50676&section=0#tabs-tree-start.



A few reminders to flag to you:



  *   If you haven’t already done so, please ensure you complete your language registration with CLIE so that your choice can be fully approved. Details on how to complete registration can be found at https://www.ucl.ac.uk/languages-international-education/language-courses/modules/modules-how-and-when-register.



  *   If you’re not registered for 120 credits, please complete your module choices ASAP. If you have any issues selecting modules yourself, please either visit the main office (room G14, 1-4 Malet Place, https://maps.ucl.ac.uk/1-4-malet-place, open 10am-12.30pm and 2-4pm, Monday to Friday) or email us at uasc-ug-office@ucl.ac.uk<mailto:uasc-ug-office@ucl.ac.uk>.



  *   A reminder of the enrolment or re-enrolment deadlines:
     *   Midday on Friday 10 October – deadline for new students to enrol
     *   Midday on Friday 24 October – deadline for continuing students (including those currently studying abroad) to re-enrol.



For continuing students who had assessments during the Late Summer Assessment period, it’s expected your progression status will be updated on Portico sometime this week.
