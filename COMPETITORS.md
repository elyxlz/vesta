# Competitors

A running list of products in Vesta's space (personal AI assistants / agents and adjacent tools).

- [OpenClaw](https://openclaw.ai/) — open-source, self-hosted personal AI assistant that bridges your messaging apps to AI coding agents.
- [Hermes Agent](https://hermes-agent.nousresearch.com/) — Nous Research's open-source persistent personal agent with memory and self-authored skills.
- [Folk](https://www.getfolk.app/) — CRM built around relationships and contacts.
- [Poke](https://poke.com/) — AI assistant you interact with over messaging.
- [Tomo](https://www.tomo.ai/) — AI personal assistant.
