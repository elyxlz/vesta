# Competitors

A running list of products in Vesta's space, grouped by how directly they compete. Vesta's
wedge: a devoted guardian (not a peer "friend"), invite-only, one dedicated machine per person.

## Consumer assistant you text (closest to Vesta)

Messaging-first, proactive, personality-forward, does real tasks. This is the crowded lane, and
most of these read alike ("friendly AI you text").

- [Poke](https://poke.com/) — iMessage/WhatsApp/Telegram assistant with recipes + proactive automations. Free/$19/$199.
- [Folk](https://www.getfolk.app/) — "your personal friend that gets anything done." iMessage/Telegram/WhatsApp, its own 24/7 computer. Free/$20/$100.
- [Tomo](https://www.tomo.ai/) — "text your Tomo," lifestyle/wellness lean, 250k+ users.
- [Martin](https://www.trymartin.com/) — text + voice + phone calls, reachable via SMS/WhatsApp/email/Slack. YC S23.
- [Orchid](https://orchid.ai/) — personal EA that lives in iMessage: texts your day, drafts replies, books meetings and doctor visits. YC.
- [Zapia](https://zapia.com/) — WhatsApp-native assistant that acts (not just replies), strong in LatAm.
- [Dola](https://heydola.com/) — AI calendar assistant on WhatsApp/Telegram/iMessage/Line, 200k+ users.
- [Memorae](https://memorae.ai/) — "memory layer above your apps": reminders, tasks, lists from WhatsApp + voice.
- [Pi](https://pi.ai/) — Inflection's conversational personal AI (more companion than task-doer, adjacent).

## Self-hosted / open-source personal agents

Runs on your machine, model-agnostic, persistent memory, self-authored skills. Sell control +
power to a technical buyer. Vesta shares the one-machine-per-person + memory ideas but is hosted
and consumer-framed.

- [OpenClaw](https://openclaw.ai/) — open-source, 29 messaging channels, browser + full system access, plugin ecosystem.
- [Hermes Agent](https://hermes-agent.nousresearch.com/) — Nous Research; persistent memory + a learning loop that writes its own skills.
- [QwenPaw](https://github.com/agentscope-ai/QwenPaw) — open-source (AgentScope, ex-CoPaw), local runtime with no API key, many chat channels.

## AI chief of staff / executive assistant (prosumer + B2B, adjacent)

Work-focused, email/calendar sorting, morning briefings, approval-gated actions.

- [Bond](https://www.bondapp.io/) — AI chief of staff for CEOs: tracks open threads, one-page daily snapshot via Slack/email.
- [Consul](https://consul.so/) — AI EA over Gmail/Calendar/Drive/Slack; scheduling, inbox sorting, follow-ups, always approval-gated.
- [Lindy](https://www.lindy.ai/) — no-code platform to build background AI agents (inbox sorting, scheduling, note-taking).

## Time / calendar tools (narrow, adjacent)

Not general assistants, but overlap on the "guard your time" job.

- [Reclaim](https://reclaim.ai/) — auto-blocks focus time, schedules habits, finds meeting slots.
- [Motion](https://www.usemotion.com/) — AI calendar + task manager that auto-plans your day.
